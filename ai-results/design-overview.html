Design 01
Data Impact

A dark, data-driven design inspired by modern NGO and tech advocacy sites. Glassmorphism header, grid overlay hero, animated bar chart, and card-based action grid. Keeps the original dark green/teal palette but dramatically more polished.

Dark theme Data-forward Glassmorphism Grid overlay Card layout
  • Sticky frosted-glass nav with logo, links, and CTA
  • Hero with grid overlay and gradient radial spotlights
  • Stats strip with tabular numerals and dividers
  • Population bar chart (CSS-only)
  • 6-card action grid with hover lift effects
  • Timeline section for achievements
  • Sticky "Sign Petition" CTA throughout

Design 02
Bold Advocate

An editorial, high-contrast design with a 50/50 split hero: narrative text on the left, dramatic statistics on the right. Lime-yellow accent on near-black creates an arresting, journalistic feel inspired by investigative media and advocacy publications.

Editorial split High contrast Serif + sans Lime accent Journalistic
  • Full-height editorial hero: text column + split statistics
  • Lime (#c6f135) accent on deep black — maximum contrast
  • DM Serif Display + DM Sans typographic pairing
  • Pull-quote mission statement section
  • Numbered 3-column info grid (bordered)
  • Full-bleed lime CTA action strip
  • Numbered help grid (01–06) with hover darkening

Design 03
Warm Nature

A warm, approachable light-theme design using cream and forest-green tones with Lora serif headlines. More inviting and accessible than the existing site, with an organic feel suited to an animal welfare NGO. Stats displayed in a compact white card bar beneath the headline.

Light theme Warm palette Serif headings Approachable Nature tones
  • Cream/warm-white background — inviting, not harsh
  • Lora serif + Source Sans 3 — warm editorial authority
  • Stats bar card directly in the hero section
  • 4 fact-cards with hover slide animation
  • Dark forest green progress banner section
  • 6-card action grid with box-shadow lift on hover
  • Story section: sidebar memorial card + narrative text

Design 04
Street Activist

Inspired by Just Stop Oil — raw, poster-like urgency with Barlow Condensed, stark black/orange contrast, a scrolling news ticker, and diagonal hero clip-path. Treats the cat crisis as a civil emergency demanding immediate action.

Dark/orange Poster energy Condensed type Ticker marquee Activist urgency
  • Scrolling orange ticker with crisis facts at top
  • Massive condensed uppercase hero headline
  • Diagonal hero clip-path for forward momentum
  • Orange stat strip with key numbers below hero
  • White high-contrast "What Must Happen" section
  • prefers-reduced-motion disables ticker animation

Design 05
Youth Movement

Inspired by Fridays For Future — a full vivid orange background floods the page with movement energy. Space Grotesk + Space Mono, bold all-caps slogan, hashtag culture, and a black stats strip below. Feels like a youth-led street protest turned website.

Full orange Movement feel Hashtag culture Black/orange Youth-led
  • Full vivid orange (#ff6600) as primary background
  • Bold all-caps slogan "NEUTER THEM ALL"
  • Hashtag #MassNeuteringNow as visual element
  • Space Mono for the data/stat strip below hero
  • Yellow info cards for action items
  • High-energy, protest-poster visual language

Design 06
Brand Pop

Inspired by Little Moons — the logo is centred in the navbar using a CSS grid 3-column layout, flanked by navigation links. A rich forest-green gradient hero with gold (#f5e642) accents, Playfair Display italic serif, and glassmorphic data cards create strong brand identity.

Centred logo nav Forest green Gold accent Italic serif Glassmorphism
  • CSS grid 1fr auto 1fr nav — centred logo (Little Moons pattern)
  • Playfair Display italic for headline authority
  • Gold #f5e642 accent on deep forest green
  • Glassmorphic hero data cards with backdrop-filter
  • SVG organic wave divider between hero and content
  • Strong brand identity front and centre
