 We have collected a large amount of data 

 about the street cats in Varna in order to prove that the 

 street cat population is increasing. Check out the results below! 

The strongest argument for improving the lives of street cats through mass neutering hinges on article 56 of the Animal Protection Act, which states:

Art. 56. (1) In cases of an increased population of stray cats, the provisions of Art. 40 - 47, Art. 51, 52 and Art. 54 shall apply.

Everyone in Varna knows that the street cat population has been increasing. But we have collected data to prove it. Check out the results below!

City district Odesos count, 2024

During October 2023 - September 2024, we counted all street cats in the Odesos region of Varna, with the help of incredible volunteers.
Total number of cats counted:
2005 street cats
Proportion of neutered cats:
17.8%

Sample counting, 2024

During October-November 2024, we counted all street cats in 15 randomly selected areas across all of Varna Municipality
Average number of cats per 10 hectares (100 decares):
20.9 cats
Proportion of neutered cats:
5.9%

Sample counting, 2025

During October-November 2024, we counted all street cats in 10 areas across all of Varna Municipality (the 5 areas with the highest numbers of street cats from the 2024 count and the 5 areas with the lowest numbers of street cats from the 2024 count)
Annual growth in the number of cats observed across the same 10 areas between 2024-2025 (= our estimate for the annual growth of the street cat population in Varna):
5.3%
