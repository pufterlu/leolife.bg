 Sign our petition! 

Please SIGN and SHARE our petition for a municipal MASS neutering programme for the street cats of Varna! According to article 56 of the Animal Protection Act, Varna Municipality MUST launch such a programme, because it has been proven that there is an "increased population of cats" in the city.

In May 2025, we launched a petition with Десети живот (Tenth life) to apply pressure on the local politicians of Varna to start a municipal MASS neutering programme for the street cats of Varna. No more ill cats and newborn kittens on the streets! 💔

Citizens and organizations are completely overwhelmed with care for animals sick with viruses, infections, poisonings, and traffic accidents. According to the municipality's own estimate, there were 23,053 cats within Varna Municipality in 2024, and the number has been growing by 85% every two years. Article 56 of the Animal Protection Act states that when there is an "increase in the population of street cats", the municipal councillors must start a programme for MASS neutering of street cats. This is THE LAW.

We surveyed 24 vets in Varna Municipality, and out of these, 19 vets stated that they are ready to work with Varna Municipality on a municipal mass neutering programme.

ENOUGH suffering on the streets! All cats deserve a life without fighting and constant births. ❤️‍🩹

We invite everyone to SIGN it, SHARE it, and be part of the change to create a better city for people and cats! ❤️🐈
